Privacy Policy

ComXpress processes your images fully in your browser. No image files are uploaded to any server by default.

Generated previews and outputs are stored locally only for your active session to support refresh recovery and user convenience.

Notification history and theme preferences are saved on your device and can be cleared by browser storage cleanup or logout actions.

By using this tool, you confirm you have rights to process uploaded photos and comply with any applicable local privacy regulations.
